About Us

The Spice food truck consists of Asian spicy food blended with organic ingredients. Our main signature dish is our Korean spicy Noodles. Our goal is to spread our spicy culture through our community and the city at large by bringing together delicious Asian food.

Founders

This food truck was first founded by Christopher Umanzor who desired to spread spicy Asian food around the city which is rare and he also had a goal to make he food truck business more diverse.
