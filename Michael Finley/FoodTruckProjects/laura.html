About Us

Mexican in a truck is a food truck that sells real authentic Mexican food. What makes us so different is that we sell authentic Mexican food and for a cheap price. We also have a veggie menu of different delicious Mexican foods. Hope you like Mexican in a truck.

Founders

This food truck was founded by a 12 year old named Laura Cottingham she wanted to sell real authentic food and for a cheap price. Laura also wanted to make healthy options for people. That is why she made a veggie menu just for our vegetable lovers.
