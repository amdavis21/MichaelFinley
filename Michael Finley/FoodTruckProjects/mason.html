About Us

Public Burger food truck is a business for all Burger lovers made with the finest freshet produce and food, I wanted to start this truck so that people can have good quality food for a good price, and so people can be happy and feel safe and secure, we also have seating area so that people can meet others, I wanted people to explore there taste buds by trying the donut burger(Do-Burger), and the reason I have this exotic burger is becuase when I was younger I always wanted mix my fovorite food together, so this burger I kind of a representation of my child hood. Don't forget to try our Sweet sauce special!

Founders

The Founder of PB's is Mason Finley, Growing up food was not what he was worried about he was worried about sports period. until he got older and his taste buds evolved and had a taste for everythng, especially burger and all kind of meats, so he thought what could he do with burgers and Public burger is where started, and then he knew burgers was definetly going to be his thing.
