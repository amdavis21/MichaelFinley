Codestream Studios Presents
Michael Finley
CodeSlam

As an introduction to STEM and coding, the Michael Finley Foundation partnered with CodeStream Studio's to present a virtual CodeSLAM. During this STEM coding event, participants received a unique 3-week virtual computer coding experience, with a focus on one pathway option: website design + development. CodeSLAMs have a unique rollout procedure and skill level approach, that prepares each coder to have a successful project and enjoyable experience at the SLAM. Events of this nature and STEM programming focused on coding, spark interest and provide the basic skills to get students on the pathway to becoming equipped for our ever-evolving digital world. These bright students ended their event by designing and building a website showcasing a Food Truck Business. The website contained the business's unique name, food menu, as well as contact information.
